Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how DS Agent ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use data-scientist-1ly9.onrender.com.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your username, email address, and a bcrypt-hashed password. We never store plain-text passwords.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you use DS Agent - queries made, datasets uploaded, features used, and session timestamps. This helps us improve the platform.

Uploaded Datasets: CSV and Excel files you upload for analysis. These are processed to provide results and stored in your account. You can delete them at any time.

Payment Information: For paid subscriptions, we collect UPI payment screenshots for manual verification. We do not store credit card numbers, bank account details, or CVV codes.

Guest Session Data: Even without registration, we collect anonymous session identifiers, IP addresses, and feature usage to improve guest experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and operate DS Agent services, process and verify payments, send important account and service notifications, analyze usage patterns to improve features, and prevent fraud and abuse of our platform.

3. Google AdSense and Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our platform. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your interests and browsing history. These cookies are set by Google, not by DS Agent. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission, bcrypt password hashing, PostgreSQL database with SSL connections, and regular security monitoring.

5. Data Retention

Account data is retained as long as your account is active. Uploaded datasets can be deleted at any time from your account. Guest session data is automatically deleted after 30 days of inactivity.
